ALL LEADERS' E-FORUM AND CD TEAM MEMBERS' ORIENTATION

The UST SIMBAHAYAN Community Development Office, in collaboration with Office for Student Affairs, annually conducts an orientation for the student organizations and community development teams of the university before the start or in the first few months of the school year to provide an overview of the University Community Development Program. It addresses the need of the student organizations as well as new CD Team members to know the processes and principles behind the conceptualization and conduct of community development activities as part of their Thomasian formation. It also serves as avenue for the expressed needs of the partner communities to be relayed to the student organizations so they may come up with program/projects/activities based on these needs.

Learn more

IMPORTANCE:

This e-forum is a means to assist the student organization officers as they create project and advocacy proposals necessary to undertake their community service activities within the academic year. This also serves as the opportunity for the organizations to know the needs of the partner communities and align their expertise in response to these needs.

OBJECTIVE:

As the student leaders and community members, participate in this event, it is hoped that the e-forum can be of help to shape them to be better leaders as the value of compassion is instilled in them. The event can also be an opportunity for the development of the student organizations into organized groups who can contribute to social transformation through the community development activities that they will organize after the e-forum/orientation.
